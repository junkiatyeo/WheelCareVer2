Q: What does the map shows?

A: It shows a map that displays your  

    current location, and the location of

    the bus stop.

Q: What does the info logo beside the bus

     service shows?

A: It shows the route of the bus service.

Q: How do I notify the bus captain for

     assistance when boarding the bus?

A: User can notify the bus captain by

    clicking on the wheelchair logo for

    assistance.

Q: What does the different color of the

     wheelchair logo represents?

A: The wheelchair lot of the arriving

    bus is empty, you may notify the

    bus captain for assistance when

    boarding

 

     The wheelchair lot of the arriving

     bus is empty, and you have

     notified the bus captain. You may

     cancel your existing notification

     by clicking on the wheelchair logo

     as well.

 

     The wheelchair lot of the arriving

     bus is occupied, please wait for

     the next bus with vacant lot.

Q: What is the Sub Bus?

A: It means the arrival time needed to reach the

    bus stop for the subsequent bus.

 

Q: What is the purpose of the refresh button?

A: It triggers the updating of the bus arrival

    times of the bus services plying along the

    bus stop.

 

Q: Why am I experiencing unavailability of data

     connection when using the application?

A: Please contact your service provider for

    further assistance.

    During the unavailability of data connection,

    users are unable to notify bus captain before

    boarding, the arrival time for both the next

    and subsequent buses will not be available.

    However, that does not affect our Journey

    Tracker functionality as information is cached

    and rely mainly on GPS.

Q: What does the map shows when selecting

     destination?

A: It shows a map that displays the

    location of the bus stop.

When on board ...

 

 Journey Tracker

 

Q: How does the Journey Tracker function?

A: It displays the location of the user and the

    list of bus stops remaining to reach the

    destination.

Giving feedback ...

 

 Feedback

 

Q: How does the Feedback features function?

A: It allows users to provide feedback on the

    use of the WheelCare application only. It

    does not include giving feedback for bus

    services.
